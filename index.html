About Us | 关于我们

Six Fortune Acupuncture and Massage is dedicated to providing high-quality Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) treatments in Melbourne. Led by Dr. Xue Yinghui, a highly experienced practitioner with over three decades in the field, our clinic focuses on a patient-centered approach to health and wellness.

Dr. Xue graduated from Liaoning University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in 1988 and has held esteemed positions, including Head of the Tuina Department at the First Affiliated Hospital of Liaoning University. He has extensive experience in treating musculoskeletal disorders, chronic pain, and various health conditions.
