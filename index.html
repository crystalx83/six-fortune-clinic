About Us | 关于我们

Six Fortune Acupuncture and Massage is dedicated to providing high-quality Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) treatments in Melbourne. Led by Dr. Xue Yinghui, a highly experienced practitioner with over three decades in the field, our clinic offers a compassionate and patient-centered approach to healthcare. Dr. Xue graduated from Liaoning University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in 1988 and has held various esteemed positions, including Head of the Tuina Department at the First Affiliated Hospital of Liaoning University. With extensive experience in treating musculoskeletal pain and chronic conditions, he brings a wealth of knowledge to every patient’s treatment plan. Our goal is to help you achieve better health and well-being through personalized, holistic therapies.
