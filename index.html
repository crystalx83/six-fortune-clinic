About Us | 关于我们

Six Fortune Acupuncture and Massage is dedicated to providing high-quality Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) treatments in Melbourne. Led by Dr. Xue Yinghui, a highly experienced practitioner with over three decades in the field, our clinic focuses on a patient-centered approach to health and wellness.

Dr. Xue graduated from Liaoning University of Traditional Chinese Medicine in 1988. He served as the Head of the Tuina Department at the First Affiliated Hospital of Liaoning University and played an important role in establishing professional Tuina therapy standards. He has extensive experience in treating musculoskeletal disorders, chronic pain, and other conditions.

Our goal is to help you achieve better health and well-being through personalized, holistic therapies. Our services are private health insurance rebatable, depending on your specific plan.

关于我们
