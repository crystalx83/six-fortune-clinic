Health Fund Rebates | 健康保险报销

Our clinic accepts health fund rebates for acupuncture and remedial massage treatments under most private health insurance providers, with the exception of HSF. Eligibility and rebate amounts depend on your individual plan. Please check with your health fund for details.

本诊所接受大多数私人健康保险的报销（HSF 除外），可用于针灸和推拿（Remedial Massage）类别的治疗。具体报销金额和资格取决于您的健康保险计划，请咨询您的保险公司获取详细信息。

Appointments | 预约

Each appointment lasts approximately one hour. For first-time visits, additional time may be needed for a consultation to understand your medical history and treatment needs. Based on your condition, treatments may include acupuncture, remedial massage, cupping, or a combination of these methods.

每次预约时长约为一小时。首次就诊可能需要额外时间进行初步咨询，以了解您的健康状况和治疗需求。根据您的具体情况，我们可能会使用针灸、推拿、拔罐或其组合进行治疗。

Facilities & Hygiene | 设施与卫生

Onsite parking is available at the clinic entrance for your convenience. All acupuncture needles used are sterile, single-use, and disposed of safely after each session to maintain the highest hygiene standards.

诊所门口提供专用停车位，方便您的到访。所有针灸针均为一次性无菌针，并在使用后安全处理，确保最高的卫生标准。
